🔧 Nosh QR — Firestore Setup Tool

This tool uploads your menu data, creates tables, and sets up the super admin. Run once.
⚠️ This uploads to the TRIAL project: nosh-qr-ordering-trial. Your live waitlist system is NOT affected.
1 Super Admin Phone Number
Your phone number — you'll use this to log into the cashier screen and menu admin.
2 Upload Everything to Firestore
This will create: 10 categories, 406 menu items, 10 tables, 1 outlet config, and your super admin account.
3 Verify Upload
Click to check that everything was uploaded correctly.
4 OTP Toggle (Testing Mode)
Turn OTP OFF for testing — customers only enter phone number, no SMS sent. Turn ON before going live.
5 Reset Admin PIN
Reset your super admin PIN to 1234. Use if you forgot your PIN or just set up the system.
! Reset (if needed)
If something goes wrong, this deletes ALL data and lets you start fresh. Use carefully.
